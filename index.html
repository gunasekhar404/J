How I Met your Mother

How I Met Your Mother is a TV comedy series that ran from 2005–2014. The show is about Ted Mosby (Josh Radnor) telling his children the story of how he met their mother, with flashbacks to his time in New York City in the 2000s. The series follows Ted and his four best friends, Marshall (Jason Segel), Lily (Alyson Hannigan), Barney (Neil Patrick Harris), and Robin (Cobie Smulders), as they navigate life's challenges and adventures.

Game Of Thrones

The series follows several simultaneous plot- lines. The first story arc follows a war of succession among competing claimants for control of the Iron Throne of the Seven Kingdoms, with other noble families fighting for independence from the throne.

Prison Break

Breaking Bad

Friends

Friends is a TV sitcom that ran from 1994–2004 and follows the lives of six friends in their 20s and 30s as they navigate love, friendship, family, and work in New York City

Titanic

James Cameron's "Titanic" is an epic, action-packed romance set against the ill-fated maiden voyage of the R.M.S. Titanic; the pride and joy of the White Star Line and, at the time, the largest moving object ever built. She was the most luxurious liner of her era -- the "ship of dreams" -- which ultimately carried over 1,500 people to their death in the ice cold waters of the North Atlantic in the early hours of April 15, 1912.

Harry Potter

Adaptation of the first of J.K. Rowling's popular children's novels about Harry Potter, a boy who learns on his eleventh birthday that he is the orphaned son of two powerful wizards and possesses unique magical powers of his own. He is summoned from his life as an unwanted child to become a student at Hogwarts, an English boarding school for wizards. There, he meets several friends who become his closest allies and help him discover the truth about his parents' mysterious deaths.
